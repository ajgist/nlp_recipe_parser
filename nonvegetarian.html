  2. 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  3. 1 and 1/2 pounds ground beef
  4. ¼ cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  5. ½ cup lemon juice
  6. ¼ cup white sugar
  7. 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  8. 1 tablespoon sriracha hot sauce
  9. 2 eggs
  10. 1 (4.5 ounce) can mushrooms
  11. ½ cup chopped cashews, divided
  12. 1 cup bean sprouts
  13. 1 lime, cut into wedges

Instructions Comparison

Before Transformation After Transformation
  1. Place shirataki noodle in a bowl of hot water ; set aside
  2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat ; cook
  3. Stir tofu until lightly browned, cooked through , 5 to 10 minute
  4. Transfer tofu to a plate , keeping skillet over heat
  5. Whisk soy sauce lemon juice , sugar , peanut butter and sriracha sauce together in a small microwave-safe bowl ; heat in microwave until peanut sauce is melted, smooth , about 20 second
  6. Stir well
  7. Cook
  8. Stir egg in the hot skillet until cooked through, scrambled , about 5 minute
  9. Mix mushroom, tofu into egg ; add 1/2 of the peanut sauce
  10. Mix well
  11. Drain shirataki noodle
  12. Add to egg mixture ; pour in the remaining peanut sauce
  13. Sprinkle 1/4 cup cashew into tofu-noodle mixture
  14. Mix well
  15. Reduce heat to medium-low
  16. Simmer until sauce thickens , 5 to 7 minute
  17. Transfer tofu-noodle mixture to a serving bowl, top with remaining cashew , bean sprout and lime wedge

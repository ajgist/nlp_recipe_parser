Instructions Comparison

Before Transformation After Transformation
  1. Heat 2 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Cook
  3. Stir carrot, green bell pepper until slightly softened , about 5 minute ; stir shrimp , onion , corn and garlic into carrot mixture
  4. Cook
  5. Stir until shrimp turn pink, are no longer translucent , 5 more minute
  6. Pour off any liquid
  7. Turn heat to low, let mixture simmer
  8. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a separate skillet over medium heat ; pour 2 beaten egg into hot oil
  9. Scramble the egg until firm, no longer runny , 2 to 3 minute
  10. Mix scrambled egg lightly into shrimp, vegetable
  11. Stir rice , oyster sauce , soy sauce , 1 tablespoon butter and salt into shrimp mixture , tossing until rice is thoroughly combined with shrimp, vegetable, coated with sauce
  12. Heat 1 teaspoon butter in a small nonstick skillet over medium heat until butter stop foaming ; break 1 of the 4 remaining egg into the hot butter
  13. Cover skillet, let egg cook until the bottom is slightly browned , the yolk is runny and the top of the white is slightly firm , about 3 minute
  14. Set egg aside
  15. Repeat with remaining 3 egg

Instructions Comparison

Before Transformation After Transformation
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degree F ( 190 degree C )
  2. Season ground beef with garlic powder
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat
  4. Cook
  5. Stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned, crumbly , 5 to 7 minute
  6. Drain
  7. Discard grease
  8. Pour spaghetti sauce , tomato sauce and oregano into a large saucepan
  9. Set aside
  10. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  11. Saute garlic, onion until softened, translucent , about 5 minute
  12. Place cooked onion-garlic mixture, cooked ground beef into the sauce mixture
  13. Cover, let simmer for 15 to 20 minute
  14. Combine mozzarella, provolone cheese in a medium bowl
  15. Mix ricotta cheese , egg , milk and 1/2 teaspoon oregano together in a separate bowl
  16. Layer a 9x13-inch baking pan with just enough sauce to cover the bottom of the pan
  17. Lay three lasagna noodle in the pan over the sauce
  18. Cover with more sauce , then with ricotta mixture then sprinkle with mozzarella/provolone mixture ; repeat layering
  19. Finish with a layer of noodle, remaining sauce
  20. Sprinkle top with Parmesan cheese
  21. Bake , covered , in the preheated oven for 30 minute
  22. Uncover
  23. Continue to bake until cheese is melted, top is golden , about 15 minute more
